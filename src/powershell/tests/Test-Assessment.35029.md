Without mail flow rules, organizations depend on users to manually apply sensitivity labels or encrypt messages. This approach can lead to inconsistencies and errors, which may result in sensitive emails being sent without proper protection and increase the risk of unauthorized access and data exfiltration.

Mail flow rules help automatically add encryption and set permissions for emails that meet certain conditions, such as:
- Mail sent to people outside the company  
- Mail that contains sensitive information  
- Mail that must follow department-based requirements  
This ensures that important messages are protected without relying on users to manually secure them.
