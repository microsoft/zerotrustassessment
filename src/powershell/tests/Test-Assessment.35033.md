Custom sensitive information types (SITs) extend Microsoft Purview's built-in detection to cover organization-specific data patterns, proprietary identifiers, internal classification schemes, specialized industry codes, or other formats that built-in SITs don't match. Without custom SITs, autolabeling policies and data loss prevention (DLP) rules rely exclusively on generic patterns and might miss sensitive data unique to your organization.

**Remediation action**

- [Create custom sensitive information types](https://learn.microsoft.com/purview/create-a-custom-sensitive-information-type?wt.mc_id=zerotrustrecommendations_automation_content_cnl_csasci)
